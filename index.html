Our Mission

Run4Fun believes physical health and education should be accessible to everyone. We hope to see exercise be an integral part in every person's life, from childhood all the way to adulthood, without being limited by cost, accessibility, and equipment.

How You Can Help

Want to get involved? Community support and participation is imperative to our goals, and any inquiries, donations, or help would be greatly appreciated. Take a look at our events and reach out to run4funwa@gmail.com if you have any questions or want to help!

About Us

Run4Fun is a youth-led nonprofit based in Bellevue, WA devoted to making exercise and health accessible to everyone in the community. By organizing community events and activities, supporting local organizations and nonprofits, collecting exercise gear and clothes and distributing to those who need it most, we seek to alleviate and target barriers to physical health and education.

Team Members

Our names are Caroline, Annabelle, Lyn, Peyton, and Connor (as shown left to right), and we are rising seniors at Newport High School. We all share a passion and background in athletics and exercise, and it is a shared goal of ours to make exercise accessible for all! We hope you join us in making our vision possible and feel free to contact us to learn more information.
